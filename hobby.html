Katy's Hobby: Computer Networks

I started learning about computer networks when I got my first internship at RTX BBN in Cambridge, MA. Since then, I've worked on several projects regarding computer networks. I hope to potentially study networks in graduate school.

Summer 2024

  • Built a 5G testbed using srsRAN and Open5GS software, and B205-mini USRPs hardware.
  • Developed a PyQt5 GUI for a cyclostationary signal processing search and discovery algorithm for displaying signal characteristics. Wrote a logger for a TDoA Matlab application.
  • Studied topology of LEO satellite constellations for understanding link strengths and routing.

Summer 2023

  • Explored Internet background radiation (packets addressed to dark networks).
  • Developed software for identifying cohorts of port scanners and anomalies in darkspace traffic.
  • Studied the distributions across autonomous systems and the geolocations of a botnet.

Summer 2022

  • Conducted experiments to study the topology of the Internet using distributed systems.
  • Created a Flask web app for managing the distributed systems.
  • Visualized geolocations of traceroute data from the distributed systems.
